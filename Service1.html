Assistance with Personal Care

Alzheimer's Disease

Maintain a higher supervision, mental comfort, and prevent over-stimulation. Companionship and easy activities to maintain a trust environment. Document and manage behavior and report changes to scheduled coordinators and supervisors. Safe transportation to doctor's appointments and maintain in-home check list for safety.

Stroke Care

Assist with daily activities and provide comfort and care during mobility, feeding, and dressing. Compassionate Companion Care.

Adults with Disabilties

Compassionate Companion Care has skilled caregivers who are ready to provide assistance with adults with disabilities. Provide a continuous secure environment. Assess the patient needs and provide to cover their needs. Assist with daily care routines such as: bathing, toileting, diapering, meal preparation, and light housekeeping.

Heart Disease

Provide medication reminders, prepare meals according patient needs, and give assistance with ADL's and daily life activities.

Dementia

Assist with personal care, provide safe environment, continuous re-orientation to facilitate patient needs and comfort care.

After-Hospitalization Care

Hygiene, errands, cleaning, feeding, comfort, and assisting the client in all areas of life possible.

Respite Care

Provide assistance to families to be able to break the daily routine with patient care and be able to do what they need most as resting, traveling, socializing, and spending more time with family and work settings. Contact us to customize your care needs.

LIVING Home Care 24 hrs of Care 7 days a Week

Provide a caregiver that is skilled to be with the client and assist 24 hours a day while knowing his personality, hobbies, likes, and dislikes. We always strive to make a comfortable and safe environment according to the client needs.
