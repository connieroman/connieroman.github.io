Our Services

We provide the following 3 services, which are scheduled to accomodate our clients and can range from 1 to 24 hours. Decide the frequency for any service and request more than one type of service.

  • Shift

    Provides personal hygiene, prepare meals, light housekeeping, errands, and companionship

    Focus is person in house.

  • Social

    Provides going out for shopping, social events, driving, and personal errands

    Focus is person outgoing activity.

  • Home

    Provides cleaning the home and light personal assistance around home

    Focus is the house.

About the company

Connie Roman is the founder and president of Compassionate Companion Care. As a Grief and Bereavement counselor, Connie observed the necessity to provide the elderly population with a service that can be empathetic to their needs, relieving their families of overwhelming duties and making life more relaxing for both parties. Connie decided to assist every single family in need and created a customized care plan that helps elderly families in need.
