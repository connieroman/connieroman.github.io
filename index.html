About the company

Connie Roman is the founder and president of Compassionate Companion Care. As a Grief and Bereavement counselor, Connie observed the necessity to provide the elderly population with a service that can be empathetic to their needs, relieving their families of overwhelming duties and making life more relaxing for both parties. Connie decided to assist every single family and created a customized care plan that financially works and helps elderly families in need.

Compassionate Companion Care has the most qualified and experienced caregivers that can be matched to every case.

State License

Compassionate Companion Care is a licensed agency with the Department of Social Services in Sacramento. Every employee has passed a live background check as required by the Department of Justice (DOJ).

All of our caregivers are registered in the Home Care Aide Registry, which is a requirement to be able to work with our company. Only caregivers that pass the live background check work with us. We provide our caregivers with initial training and they undergo annual training requirements.

We follow all COVID-19 requirements as needed. We comply with the T.B. testing requirement every two years.

Furthermore, we carry a general liability insurance coverage up to $3,000,000, with up to $1,000,000 per occurence. Bonded with Surety.
